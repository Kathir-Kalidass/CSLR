\section{Advantages and Limitations of Existing Approaches}
Advantages:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
\item High accuracy due to deep feature learning,
\item Ability to handle complex gesture patterns,
\item Improved temporal modeling.
\end{itemize}

Limitations:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
\item High computational complexity,
\item Dependency on large datasets,
\item Limited focus on real-time performance,
\item Insufficient research on Indian Sign Language.
\end{itemize}
