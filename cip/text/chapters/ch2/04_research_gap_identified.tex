\section{Research Gap Identified}
From the literature, the following gaps are identified:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
\item Lack of efficient real-time ISL recognition systems,
\item Limited use of feature fusion techniques,
\item High computational overhead in advanced models,
\item Insufficient datasets and benchmarks for ISL.
\end{itemize}

These gaps highlight the need for a lightweight and efficient hybrid model for practical ISL recognition.
