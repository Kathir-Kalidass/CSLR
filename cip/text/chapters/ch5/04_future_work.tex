\section{Future Work}
The proposed system can be further enhanced in the following ways:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
\item Extension to continuous sign language recognition and full sentence translation,
\item Integration of multimodal inputs such as pose landmarks and depth data,
\item Development of a mobile or embedded application for real-world deployment,
\item Improvement of dataset size and diversity for better generalization,
\item Incorporation of advanced Transformer-based architectures for higher accuracy.
\end{itemize}
