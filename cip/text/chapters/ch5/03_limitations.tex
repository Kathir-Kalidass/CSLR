\section{Limitations}
Despite promising results, the system has certain limitations:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
\item The system is limited to isolated gesture recognition and does not support continuous sentence-level translation,
\item Performance depends on dataset quality and size,
\item Computational requirements can be high for low-end devices,
\item Accuracy may reduce for complex gestures or noisy environments.
\end{itemize}
